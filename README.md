# Metal Forging: Complete Study Notes

A comprehensive, interactive web-based study guide for **Metal Forging** — covering processes, formulas, equipment, die design, defects, and exam-ready content.

## 📋 Features

- **9 Comprehensive Topics**
  - 01 Introduction & History
  - 02 Forging Types (Cold, Warm, Hot)
  - 03 Force Analysis & Kf Factor
  - 04 Additional Operations (Edging, Fullering, Cogging)
  - 05 Equipment (Hammers, Presses, Furnaces)
  - 06 Die Design & Flash
  - 07 Common Defects & Prevention
  - 08 Formula Sheet (8 Key Equations)
  - 09 Interactive Quiz (10 Questions)

- **Interactive Elements**
  - Tabbed navigation for easy topic switching
  - Collapsible type details
  - 10-question quiz with instant feedback & scoring
  - Progress scroll bar
  - Exam tips & memory aids throughout

- **Professional Design**
  - Dark-themed, modern UI
  - Responsive grid layouts (works on mobile & desktop)
  - Color-coded cards (Amber, Teal, Coral, Blue, Green, Purple)
  - Formula highlighting with proper notation
  - Optimized typography (Crimson Pro, Sora, DM Mono fonts)

## 📚 Content Highlights

### Key Topics Covered
- **Forging Definition & History** — From 4000 BCE to modern manufacturing
- **Temperature Classification** — Cold, warm, and hot forging techniques
- **Force Analysis** — Kf factor calculations and flow stress
- **Die Design** — Flash design, complex shapes, precision control
- **Defects** — Causes and prevention (laps, folds, cold shuts, scale pits)
- **Equipment** — Board drop hammers, hydraulic presses, mechanical hammers

### Learning Resources
- 8 critical formulas for exams
- 15+ exam tips highlighted throughout
- Worked examples and calculations
- Comparison tables (Kf values, defect types, equipment specs)
